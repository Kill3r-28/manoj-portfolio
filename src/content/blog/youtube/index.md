# Youtube
Everybody's favourite.

There's no coming back once you go through the magic door.

A new economy has been built over it.

There's no denying its benefits. But just like anything else in life, it too has a limit.

And it exceeded a limit in my life.

In my college I was called the Youtube binge watcher and I used to take pride in it, partly because most of the content I watched was related to Physics or International Relations or News analysis and other pomp subjects. 

Recently, I've started finding Youtube rather distracting.

When I wanted to check a recipe for Dal Kichdi and open youtube there's page full of interesting content screaming for my attention and my enchanted thumb simply gives up and clicks on the first thumbnail.

"Just this one" I tell myself and go ahead. After some clicks I find myself in a completely new world and forget that all I wanted was Dal Kichdi !

This has been happening to me many times recently and I was looking for a solution to this problem in the back of my head.

Then, I came across this video:

![Screenshot or video](https://d3f4ult1.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/image.png?w=536)

Ofcourse this recommendation was typical of my watch history. And I watched it.

She gave an excellent suggestion. And with just two clicks I triumphantly changed the way I experience life!

Nope, not exaggerating.

That suggestion was,

![Suggestion screenshot](https://d3f4ult1.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/image-1.png?w=654)

There are two little buttons whimpering **"Clear all watch history"** and **"Turn off watch history"**

I went ahead and cleared my **17 YEARS LONG WATCH HISTORY!**

And now, my Youtube page looks like this,

![Youtube page](https://d3f4ult1.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/image-2.png?w=1024)

Best part is, all my subscriptions are just lying there! All my yesteryear's favourites.

Now I simply go through those channels and pick up whatever I want, just like good old Television.

Now there's nothing stopping me from catching that Dal Kichdi recipe in one go!

Thanks Youtube :)
